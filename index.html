Serving the Panhandle Since 2003

What started as a passion for fine blades has grown into West Texas's most trusted knife destination. With locations in both Amarillo and Lubbock, we've spent over two decades connecting people with the perfect blade.

Whether you're a chef seeking the perfect kitchen knife, a collector hunting rare pieces, or looking for self-defense gear — we're your people.
